## Project focus

SMART-LEARN aimed to design learning environments that better address individual learning paths, diverse prior knowledge, and varying learning speed.

## Approach

In pilot courses, didactic concepts, learning analytics, and adaptive content were combined and tested with students from multiple departments.

## Results

The project provides transferable design patterns for digital teaching and demonstrates how adaptive learning offers can be embedded in higher education in a didactically meaningful and technically robust way.
