## Project focus

KIAP examines how AI-supported assistance systems can improve daily work in SMEs while staying usable, trusted, and responsibly designed.

## Approach

The project combines qualitative interviews, company case studies, and prototype testing. Employees, managers, and technical experts are involved throughout the process.

## Expected impact

The project delivers practical implementation roadmaps, recommendations for qualification and training, and design principles for human-centered AI deployment.
