## Project focus

INNOV-HEALTH explores how patients and medical professionals can be systematically involved in innovation processes for digital health services.

## Approach

Using living-lab formats and method pilots, digital solutions are co-designed, tested, and iteratively improved with relevant stakeholders.

## Expected impact

The project establishes robust methods for patient-centered innovation and strengthens the practical fit of digital health tools in real care settings.
