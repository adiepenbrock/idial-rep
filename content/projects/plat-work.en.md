## Project focus

The project investigates how job quality, social protection, and participation evolve in platform-based forms of work.

## Approach

PLAT-WORK combines empirical data analysis, interviews, and participatory workshops to connect perspectives from practice, policy, and research.

## Expected impact

Findings provide an evidence-based foundation for fair platform labor and actionable guidance for regulation, organizational standards, and sustainable work models.
