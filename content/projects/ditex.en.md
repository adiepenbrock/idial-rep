## Project focus

DiTEx examined how local retail companies can use digital technologies to strengthen competitiveness and improve customer experience in a sustainable way.

## Approach

The project team collaborated with regional businesses through multiple analysis and development cycles, evaluating technology options in real operational contexts.

## Results

The project produced practical transformation guides for SMEs, especially for process digitalization, data literacy, and integrated customer journeys across channels.
