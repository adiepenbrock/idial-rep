## Project focus

AMR-NRW addresses core questions around autonomous mobile systems in industrial environments: safety, economic viability, and effective human-robot collaboration.

## Approach

Together with regional companies, the team models realistic use cases, builds prototypes, and evaluates deployment scenarios under practical conditions.

## Expected impact

The project provides transferable technical and organizational solutions that support step-by-step, safe integration of robotics into business processes.
